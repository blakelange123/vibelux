Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I start seeing savings? +
Energy savings begin immediately upon installation. Rebate processing typically takes 60-90 days, though some programs offer faster timelines. We provide bridge financing options if needed.
What if I've already upgraded my lighting? +
We can still help! Our optimization algorithms and monitoring can increase efficiency by 15-25% even with newer systems. Plus, you may qualify for retroactive rebates or performance-based incentives.
Do you work with specific fixture brands? +
We're brand-agnostic and work with all major manufacturers. Our platform integrates with any DLC-listed fixtures and can optimize performance regardless of brand.
What happens if rebate programs change? +
Our team continuously monitors all rebate programs and adjusts strategies accordingly. We guarantee to maximize available incentives and will identify new opportunities as they arise.
Is there a minimum facility size? +
We typically work with facilities over 10,000 sq ft, but we evaluate each opportunity individually. Smaller facilities can often be bundled for portfolio-level savings.
